Total Office Products Solutions

TopForm's Total Office Products Solutions (TOPS) module enables distributors to process customer orders for United Stationers office supply products directly through TopForm software. TopForm users who are subscribing dealers with United Stationers, and use a separate application to process United Stationers orders, have two major concerns:

  • The duplication of effort required to enter the order data in the third party application and re-enter it in TopForm

  • The inherent risk of data-entry errors when manually transferring order data from one application to another

TopForm developed the TOPS module to eliminate the need for a third-party order processing application for United Stationers orders and solve these two distributor problems. See the TOPS online Help or user guide for more information.
