Chart of Accounts

 

DB Distributor > General Ledger > Reports > Chart of Accounts (G/L R1)

 

The Chart of Accounts report is used to obtain a listing of your general ledger accounts that have been setup and their descriptions.

 

Follow these steps to run this report:

  1. Enter in starting and ending general ledger account numbers to print the report for only a portion of your accounts, or set to FIRST and LAST to receive a listing of all your accounts. Note: Press the F10 key in CHUI to default the fields to FIRST and LAST. In GUI, you can do this by emptying the field and then pressing enter.
  2. Modify the Output Options as needed to select where the report should print.
  3. Press the Print Report button.
