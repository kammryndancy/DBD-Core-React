AR Invoice Entry - Summary Screen

 

DB Distributor > Accounts Receivable > Invoicing > Invoice Entry (A/R I1)

 

The Invoice Summary Screen is used to verify the details of your invoice. On this panel you will want to make sure that your net sale, tax amounts, freight amount, and invoice total are correct. You can also review you cost, gross profit, and commission information to verify the numbers are acceptable.

 

If anything on this panel appears incorrect, you can use the Back button to return to the Invoice Lines section to make corrections. If everything looks correct, you can use the Next button to continue to the GL Distribution.
