Specific Finance Charge Entry

 

DB Distributor > Accounts Receivable > Period End > Finance Charge Application > Specific Posting (ARP F2)

 

The Specific Finance Charge Entry screen is used for editing any of the finance charges that were calculated by the Finance Charge Calculation program.

 

Follow these steps to complete the panel:

  1. Select the fiscal year and accounting period to be edited.
  2. Select the invoice number that needs to be edited.
  3. Edit the finance charge amount, or press the Delete button to remove the finance charge.
  4. If you have changed the finance charge amount, press the Proceed button to make the necessary GL entry.
  5. Press Save.

 

Continue to Invoice Printing if you wish to print invoices for the finance charges, or continue to the Finance Charge Update.
