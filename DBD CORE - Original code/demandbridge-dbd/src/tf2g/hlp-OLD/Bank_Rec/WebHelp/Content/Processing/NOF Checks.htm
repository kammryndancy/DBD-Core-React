Bank Statement Data - NOF Checks

 

DB Distributor > Bank Reconciliation > Processing > Bank Statement Data > Checks (B/R P1 [F8])

 

This is the area of the bank reconciliation where you will come to account for checks or deposits that have been cleared by the bank, but were processed outside of DB Distributor. These are known as Not on File (NOF) transactions. Note: NOF transactions are noted in the Bank Rec system, and on reports, but are not updated automatically in AP, AR, or GL. It will be necessary for you to decide if a Journal Entry is appropriate, or if you will need to make an entry in AP or AR.

 

Follow these steps to complete the panel:

 

  1. Enter in the check number, or deposit reference number, of the NOF transaction.
  2. Enter in the date the transaction cleared the bank.
  3. Enter in a description for the transaction.
  4. Enter in the amount of the transaction, using the minus sign for deposits.
  5. Press the Save button to save the record.
  6. After entering in all of your NOF transactions, press the Exit button.
