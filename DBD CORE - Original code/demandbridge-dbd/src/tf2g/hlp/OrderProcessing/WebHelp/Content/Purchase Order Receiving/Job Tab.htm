Job Tab

 

DB Distributor > Order Processing > Purchase Orders > Processing > Purchase Order Receiving > Job Tab (P/O P3)

 

Use the Job Tab on the P/O Receiving Panel to enter job information for the purchase order.

 

  1. Optionally, enter the Job Number, Job Date, Ending Number, and/or Combination Quantity for the purchase order.
  2. Click Finish. If the line was designated with special shipping in Order Entry, the PO Special Shipping Receiving Panel is displayed, followed by the Inventory Lot Receiving Panel. If special shipping was not designated on the original order lines, the Lines Tab is redisplayed.
