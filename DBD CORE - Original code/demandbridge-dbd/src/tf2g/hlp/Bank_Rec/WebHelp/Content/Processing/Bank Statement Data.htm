Bank Statement Data

 

DB Distributor > Bank Reconciliation > Processing > Bank Statement Data (B/R P1)

 

The Bank Statement Data screen is where you will come to reconcile your bank statement. Here you will clear checks, clear deposits, and enter in adjustments. Follow the steps below to complete the panel:

 

  1. Select the bank code you would like to reconcile.
  2. Enter in the period ending date for the period you are reconciling.
  3. The beginning balance will default to the ending balance of the previous month. If this is your first month to reconcile, it will set itself to the ending balance you set in the bank account setup screen, step number 6.
  4. Enter in the total of deposits from the statement, followed by the actual number of deposits.
  5. Enter in the total of checks and other debits from the statement, followed by the actual number of checks/debits.
  6. Enter in the dollar amount of any adjustments and service fees.
  7. You will then need to use a combination of the following 4 options (buttons) to reconcile your statement:
  • Check
  • Deposit
  • Adjustment
  • NOF Check
  1. While reconciling your balance, using the options from step 7, you will notice that the Amount to Reconcile in the lower right corner will be changing. The values in the Working Totals section (determined by the amounts entered in steps 4 through 6) will also be changing. Your goal is to bring these figures to zero. If you have cleared all of your items and made all adjustments and you are still not at zero, please refer to the Resolving Differences topic in the How To... section.
  2. If your Working Totals and Amount to Reconcile has reached zero, you will want to press the Save button, and then Exit. You will then proceed to the Bank Reconciliation Report and Update.
